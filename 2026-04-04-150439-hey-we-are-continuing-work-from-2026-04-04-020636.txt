           + 3.5) --- This algorithm describes the online calibration pro
           +cedure for per-entry conformal similarity thresholds. When a 
           +feedback signal is received for a cached entry, the algorithm
           + first computes a nonconformity score based on the similarity
           + value and whether the cache hit was correct or incorrect. Fo
           +r correct hits, the nonconformity score is simply $1 - \text{

           +imilarity. For incorrect hits, the score is inflated using th
           +e formula $\min(1.0, \max(1.0 - \text{similarity} + 0.1, 0.5)

           + then set to $1.0$ minus this quantile value, clamped to the

           +ld provided by the Thompson Sampling bandit. This procedure p
